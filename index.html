About

El Greco opened it's doors for the first time the summer of 2011 with a love for the good food. We are a family business that relies on quality, taste and great client service. It's been 12 years since Pavlina's commitment to provide you with the best products has allowed "El Greco" to make it's tables every day and welcome you as if we were inviting you into our own homes, so we could all enjoy this meal together.

Kontosouvli

Pork & Chicken Kontosouvli, a local delicacy that you simply cannot miss. Here you can find it slowly roasted for you to fully enjoy it's traditional greek flavours. Enjoy it in a sanswich or a portion with a side of crispy french fries, sala and cornbread pita.

Gyros

Well done and decatently tasty, the pork gyros is prepared with an old traditional recipe that guarantees a unique taste. Enjoy it in a sanswich or a portion with a side of crispy french fries, sala and cornbread pita.

Chicken & Pork Souvlaki

The 100gr, well mmarinated pork and chicken souvlaki is full of flavours you have never seen before. Enjoy it in a sanswich or a portion with a side of crispy french fries, sala and cornbread pita.
